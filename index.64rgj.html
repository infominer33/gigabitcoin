How does the Lightning network work in simple terms?

Say, you and I want to open a payment channel. 1) Putting funds into Lightning We both send 0.05 BTC into a shared 2-of-2 multisig address. This requires a transaction on the Bitcoin blockchain. As a "balance sheet", we each create a 2-of-2 multisig transaction that pays out 0.05 BTC to you and 0.05 BTC to me.

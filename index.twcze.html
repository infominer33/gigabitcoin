In an effort to improve transparency in clinical trial research, two doctors created a system that coverts trial documents into a Bitcoin - an online equivalent of cash. The new approach, which prevents clinical trial documents from being altered to make new medications look more effective, was developed by Greg Irving, of the University of Cambridge, and John Holden - both practicing doctors concerned about the trustworthiness of clinical trials.

The Halvening! Bitcoin Halving for Rocket Lovers

In the Bitcoin network, user transactions are grouped in blocks and recorded to a digital public ledger called a blockchain. Miners are in charge of this task, and receive a mining reward in the form of bitcoins for each block recorded. The amount of bitcoins rewarded for each block decreases with time: it is halved every 4 years.

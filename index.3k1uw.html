Venezuela's Biggest Bitcoin Exchange Resumes Operations

The Bitcoin exchange announced the successful relaunch of its Fiat gateway.

Moody's Investors Service (MIS) recently released a detailed report that names 120 blockchain projects being explored by various companies. The report, "Credit Strategy -- Blockchain Technology: Robust, Cost-effective Applications Key to Unlocking Blockchain's Potential Credit Benefits," explores how blockchain tech potentially can improve record-keeping and transactional efficiencies across many different processes and industries.

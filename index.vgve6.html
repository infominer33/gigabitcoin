Making Sense of Bitcoin's Reward Halving - CoinDesk

Of all the rules in bitcoin's code, few are as revered as the hard limit of bitcoin production. The code dictates that 21 million coins will be released over the course of bitcoin's lifecycle. By limiting the total amount of bitcoins that could be created, Satoshi Nakamoto was able to establish a defined amount of available data, a revolutionary accomplishment in and of itself.
